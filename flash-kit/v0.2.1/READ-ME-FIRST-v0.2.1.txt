KERNEL v0.2.1 - THE WIFI FIX ATTEMPT (READ ME FIRST)
=====================================================

WHAT THIS IS
------------
v0.1 broke WiFi because it was built from a NEWER kernel
(6.6.139) than your phone's stock one (6.6.102). Your phone's
WiFi driver checks exact version stamps and refused to load.

v0.2.1 is built from the EXACT same source snapshot Google used
for your stock kernel: 6.6.102-android15-8, same compiler, same
settings. The WiFi driver should now accept it.

This kit is the same routine you already did once (and undid
once) - flash boot only, in fastbootd. Root stays. Undo included.

HOW TO USE
----------
1. Unzip this WHOLE zip into your existing kernel-kit folder
   (the one with the platform-tools folder and backup\ folder
   from last time). Say YES to overwrite boot.img.
   - The backup\boot_stock_a.img from STEP 1 must still be
     there. If it is missing, STOP and tell Viktor.
2. Phone ON, unlocked, USB cable in, USB debugging ON.
3. Double-click  STEP-2-FASTBOOTD-FLASH.bat  and follow it.
4. After reboot, check in this order:
     - WiFi turns on and connects   <-- the big one
     - Magisk still shows root
     - Camera, fingerprint, a short call
     - Phone not hot
5. Copy the window text + your checks to Viktor - pass or fail.

IF WIFI IS STILL DEAD (or anything else is wrong):
   Double-click  UNDO-FASTBOOTD.bat  - same undo you already
   used successfully. You'll be back on stock in 2 minutes.

IF THE PHONE WON'T BOOT:
   - Hold POWER ~10s to force off.
   - Hold VOLUME DOWN + plug in USB -> bootloader fastboot.
   - Double-click  UNDO-FASTBOOTD.bat

Nothing here touches init_boot, vendor_boot, thermal, or your
data/banking. Root (Magisk) is preserved.
